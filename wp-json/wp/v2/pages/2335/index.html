    <\/span>How do I purchase a product?<\/span><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    Find the product you wish to purchase in the store<\/a>, click the Buy button, and select your subscription\u00a0or package. You\u2019ll then be taken to our checkout page where you can log into your existing account to use saved information or register a new account. The checkout process is below the register area.
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    <\/span><\/a>I Haven\u2019t Received My Receipt Or Login Details?<\/span><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    The e-mail is sent the moment your payment is approved, and that should be almost immediately. If you don\u2019t receive an email receipt or login information soon after, please\u00a0contact us<\/a>\u00a0and we\u2019ll check on your payment and account.
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    <\/span><\/a>I Can\u2019t Log Into My Account!<\/span><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    If you are having trouble logging into your account, please\u00a0contact us<\/a>\u00a0and we\u2019ll sort it out.
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    <\/span><\/a>I Can\u2019t Find My Products!<\/span><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    If you don\u2019t see your purchased products on the\u00a0My Account<\/a>\u00a0page, please\u00a0contact us<\/a>. If you have another account or bought a product on behalf of a client, be prepared to provide that information.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    <\/span><\/a>How Do I Buy A Product With My Existing Account?<\/span><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    Ensure that you are logged into the correct account before going to the checkout page. If you aren\u2019t logged in, or you\u2019re logged into the wrong account, please log into the correct account before starting the purchase.
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    <\/span><\/a>Which Payment Types Do You Accept?<\/span><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    We currently accept all major payment cards and use Paypal as our payment gateway: Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover. All prices are in charged in US Dollars (USD) and this will be exchanged to your local currency by your bank or card issuer.
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    <\/span><\/a>I Can\u2019t Purchase Anything\/There Is An Error At Checkout!<\/span><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    Please\u00a0contact us<\/a>, and we\u2019ll be happy to find out what happened.
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    <\/span><\/a>Where Do I Download My Purchase(s)?<\/span><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    Once your order is complete, a download link is shown in the Downloads tab of your My Account page, you will also receive an email notification with a download link. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
