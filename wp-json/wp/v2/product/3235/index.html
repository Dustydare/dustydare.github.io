This is an up-to-date and comprehensive book about the history of Richwood, Ohio since its founding in 1832. It contains the history of the state of Ohio, Union County, Claibourne Township and Richwood itself. The book was made possible thanks to the various people of Richwood who took the time to research about the village as well as kept photos of Richwood’s past. This was a collaborative effort made by the people of Richwood to keep its history alive and well.<\/p>\n

All proceeds of the book will be donated to the restoration efforts of the Richwood Opera House.<\/em><\/p>\n
